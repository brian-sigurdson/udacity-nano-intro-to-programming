Ursus arctos

Brown bears aren't just brown, they can also be a cream color or even black.

  • Scientific Name: Ursus arctos
  • Common Name: Brown Bear, Grizzly Bear
  • Color: Brown, Black, Cream
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years

Brown bears are found in many parts of the world, such as: Canada, Alaska, Russia, and Finland. They have an average lifespan of 25 years for males and female, but can live to be as much as 47 years old. They are omnivore, which means that they survive by eating both plant and animal matter.
